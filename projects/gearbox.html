Airplane Gearbox Design

Fully designed an in-depth model gearbox for a small airplane, given life and loading parameters using engineering design optimization principles.

The Problem

Small aircraft require efficient, reliable gearbox systems to transfer power from the engine to the propeller while meeting strict weight, safety, and performance requirements. The challenge involved:

  • Weight constraints: Every gram matters in aircraft design - gearbox must be lightweight
  • Safety requirements: Must withstand extreme loads and fatigue cycles without failure
  • Efficiency optimization: Minimize power losses while maintaining reliability
  • Manufacturing feasibility: Design must be cost-effective to produce
  • Maintenance accessibility: Components must be serviceable in field conditions

I needed to design a complete gearbox system that would meet all performance criteria while staying within strict weight and cost limitations for a small aircraft application.

The Approach

I approached this design challenge using systematic engineering methodology, combining theoretical analysis with advanced CAD modeling and FEA simulation to optimize the gearbox design.
