2016 Election Tweet Analysis

Data science project analyzing the frequency of Trump mentions by bots during the 2016 presidential election. The project involved scraping Twitter data, cleaning duplicate tweets (removing retweets), and analyzing bot behavior patterns. Results showed approximately 11-15% of tweets were duplicates, which were cleaned to provide accurate analysis.
