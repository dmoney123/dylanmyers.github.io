Rook Salt Shaker

Multi-functional chess piece-inspired salt shaker designed and manufactured using Design for Manufacturing (DFM) principles.

Project Description

This project involved designing and manufacturing a functional salt shaker inspired by a chess rook piece. The design emphasizes both aesthetic appeal and practical functionality, incorporating Design for Manufacturing (DFM) principles throughout the development process.

The salt shaker features a hollow interior with strategically placed holes for salt dispensing, while maintaining the distinctive silhouette of a chess rook. The design process included 3D modeling in SOLIDWORKS, toolpath generation in MASTERCAM, and careful consideration of manufacturing constraints and material properties.

The final product demonstrates successful integration of form and function, showcasing practical application of engineering design principles in creating a manufacturable consumer product.
